6 topics +互动
01 · Celebration Style / 庆祝方式

🕯️ PAST 过去

🎄 Simple home-based family gatherings, intimate moments.

简单家庭庆祝，亲密的家庭时光

👪 Small gatherings with close relatives & neighbors.

与近亲和邻居的小型聚会

🕯️ Cozy atmosphere: candles, fireplaces, handmade decorations.

温馨氛围：蜡烛、壁炉、手工装饰

✨ NOW 现在

🎉 Big parties at restaurants, hotels, public venues.

餐厅/酒店/公共场所的大型派对

🛍️ Shopping mall decorations, light shows.

商场装饰、灯光秀

📸 Instagram-worthy events, photo spots everywhere.

适合拍照打卡的活动

❓ How does YOUR family celebrate? At home or outside?
你的家庭怎么庆祝？
02 · Food / 食物

🍲 PAST

🍗 Home-cooked meals with family recipes.

家庭烹饪 · 祖传食谱

🍮 Traditional: roast turkey, Christmas pudding, gingerbread.

传统：烤火鸡、圣诞布丁、姜饼

🍔 NOW

🍽️ Restaurant dining / fancy Christmas menus.

餐厅就餐 · 高档圣诞餐

📱 Fast food & online delivery — order with a click.

快餐 & 外卖 · 一键下单

🍴 Favourite Christmas food? Cook at home or eat out?
你最爱的圣诞食物？在家做还是外出？
03 · Gifts / 礼物

🎁 PAST

❤️ Handmade, thoughtful presents — love and effort.

手工制作 · 充满心意

🎀 Meaningful — it's the thought that counts.

有意义的礼物 · 心意无价

💰 NOW

💵 Money / gift cards — practical but less personal.

现金/礼品卡 · 实用但缺少个性

👟 Expensive branded items, latest gadgets.

昂贵品牌商品、最新电子产品

🎁 Handmade or store‑bought? Which do you prefer?
手工还是商店购买？你喜欢哪种？
04 · Religion / 宗教

✝️ PAST

⛪ Strong religious focus — celebrating Jesus' birth.

宗教意义浓厚 · 庆祝耶稣诞生

📖 Midnight mass, Bible stories, hymns.

午夜弥撒、圣经故事、赞美诗

🛍️ NOW

📦 Commercial — Black Friday, Christmas sales.

商业化——黑色星期五、圣诞促销

🏬 Less religious meaning for many.

许多人庆祝但没有宗教意义

❓ Is the religious meaning still important? Why?
圣诞节的宗教意义还重要吗？
05 · Technology / 科技

✉️ PAST

📝 Handwritten letters to Santa, storytelling.

手写信给圣诞老人 · 讲故事

👥 Face-to-face time without distractions.

面对面交流，没有干扰

📱 NOW

🛒 Online shopping, buy gifts anytime.

网购 · 随时随地买礼物

📸 Social media (IG, TikTok) & video calls with family.

社交媒体分享 & 视频通话

💬 Does tech make Christmas better or worse?
科技让圣诞更好还是更糟？
06 · Summary / 对比总结

🎄 PAST

🏠 Simple · intimate · home‑centered

✝️ Religious focus

💝 Handmade gifts · home cooking

🎉 NOW

🏬 Big, public · commercial

💰 Shopping focus

📱 Tech‑driven · expensive gifts

🔑 Key changes: Private → Public · Religious → Commercial · Handmade → Store‑bought · Simple → High‑tech
Which changes are GOOD? What would you keep from the past?
哪些变化是好的？你想保留过去的什么？

❤️ But one thing never changes · 但有一件事从未改变 ❤️

LOVE · FAMILY · GIVING

最好的圣诞礼物不在树下，而在所爱之人的心中。

"The best Christmas gift is not found under the tree, but in the hearts of those we love."
